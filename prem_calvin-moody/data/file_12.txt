sfogliatelle - the riccia

dough has to be glass thin. semolina and ricotta filling.
- lard for the lamination, not butter. grandma was right again
- rest the roll overnight, cut it cold
- fan the layers before you fill or they wont open in the oven
- 425 hot, steam the first 5 min

pumpkin version for the family - work some roasted squash into the semolina,
cinnamon, pull the sugar back. test it the 11th.
